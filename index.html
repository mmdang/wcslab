About WCS Lab

Advancing wireless communication theory and systems

WCS Lab develops analytical models and practical insights for next-generation wireless communication systems, focusing on secure, reliable, and intelligent networks.

🔐

Secure Communications

Information-theoretic security, secrecy capacity, secrecy outage probability, and eavesdropping models.

📡

Wireless Systems

MIMO, beamforming, antenna selection, user scheduling, relay networks, and diversity combining.

🧠

Intelligent Networks

RIS-assisted communications, 6G wireless systems, AI-enabled communication technologies, and V2X.

Research Areas

Core research topics

🛡️

Physical Layer Security

Secrecy performance analysis in fading channels, imperfect CSI, multiple eavesdroppers, and secure diversity schemes.

🪞

RIS-assisted Communications

Secure RIS-aided transmission, path/RIS selection, phase errors, and performance analysis.

📶

MIMO Communications

MU-MIMO, MRT, ZF, TAS, MRC, SC, generalized selection combining, and scheduling algorithms.

🔁

Relay Networks

AF/DF relay systems, relay-user selection, dual-hop secrecy, and Nakagami/Rayleigh fading analysis.

🚗

V2X Communications

Secure and reliable communication technologies for vehicle-to-everything systems and standards.

✨

AI-enabled Wireless Systems

Emerging research on AI-assisted radio networks, intelligent propagation, and future 6G systems.

Director

Professor Donghun Lee

Dr. Donghun Lee received his Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering from Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST). His research expertise includes MIMO technologies for wireless communications, physical layer security, V2X systems, spectrum sharing, and wireless performance analysis.

Before joining Kongju National University, he worked at Chosun University, the Korean Agency for Technology and Standards, ETRI, and LG Innotek/LIG Nex1, gaining broad experience in academia, public research, standardization, and industry.
